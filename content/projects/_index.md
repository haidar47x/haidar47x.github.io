## Pocketer

Pocketer is a Google Chrome extension that lets you quickly manage your Pocket bookmarks and read-later items easily inside the browser. It's inspired by the [In My Pocket](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/in-my-pocket/) add-on, which is only available on Mozilla Firefox.

The official Pocket extension only lets you add webpages to Pocket, which is too impractical for my use.

It's available on [Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/pocketer/gkamgpflmkdbicikhmbdadddfmgeiill) and [GitHub](https://github.com/haidar47x/pocketer).
